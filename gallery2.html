Dates

George Alfred Greenslade was born on October 18, 1864 in Bishops Nympton, county of Devon, England.

He died on July 6, 1947 in Salisbury, Maryland.

About These Photographs

These photographs were taken about 1911, when George Alfred Greenslade was working on the Madeira-Mamoré Railway in Brazil. It is not known who took the photos, although the notations that appear on the back of some of the photos are believed to have been made by George Alfred Greenslade.
