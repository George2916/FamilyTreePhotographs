About These Photos

The large photograph below hung in the Duffy family apartment for many years. It features photos of the six Duffy children taken at the time of their first communion, photos of a young Edward Joseph Duffy and Mary "Mae" Ellen Torpey, and a wedding photo of the latter two individuals.
